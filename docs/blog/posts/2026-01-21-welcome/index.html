Building a fast personal site

Why I kept this site static, tiny, and incredibly quick.

I wanted this site to feel instant, so I made it static and kept the surface area small. No heavy frameworks, no extra scripts, and just enough CSS to keep the layout clean.

A few principles I’m following:

  • Ship HTML first, then sprinkle enhancements.
  • Keep CSS lean and predictable.
  • Optimize media before it ever touches the site.
  • Avoid unnecessary dependencies.

The goal is the same every week: publish on schedule without slowing the site down.
